## What Is Content Rewards

Content Rewards is a brand-funded content platform where brands post campaigns with defined budgets and pay creators per verified 1,000 views. Unlike traditional influencer marketing, Content Rewards eliminates pitch decks, rate negotiations, and invoice chasing. A creator submits content that fits a brief, the brand approves it, and the creator earns per view at a predetermined rate.

Individual campaign budgets regularly clear $100,000. The platform is open to faceless, AI-generated, and clipping-style content — precisely the output of the CorCompass Content Engine pipeline.

## Campaign Matching Protocol

Lumen conducts a bi-weekly Content Rewards brief scan. The matching process follows this sequence:

**Step 1 — Browse Active Campaigns**  
Review all active campaigns on Content Rewards. Note the brand, category, payout rate (per 1,000 views), campaign budget remaining, and content requirements.

**Step 2 — Category Matching**  
Filter campaigns by the four matching categories: AI content, clipping, faceless, music/audio. Discard campaigns requiring on-camera talent or branded product placement that conflicts with the faceless pipeline format.

**Step 3 — Topic Cluster Alignment**  
For each matching campaign, identify which pre-loaded topic cluster produces content that fits the brief. A streaming platform campaign may align with Film Production History or TV Show Retrospectives. A consumer goods campaign may align with Cultural Anniversaries or Throwback Compilations.

**Step 4 — Brief Compliance Check**  
Verify that the selected topic cluster content does not conflict with the brand's content guidelines. Some campaigns prohibit competitor mentions, political content, or content involving specific public figures.

**Step 5 — Pipeline Redirect**  
Redirect one or more pipeline sessions toward the matched topic cluster. Generate 10–20 scripts per session targeting the brief. Submit the finished videos through the Content Rewards platform.

## Payout Structure

Content Rewards pays per verified 1,000 views. Payout rates vary by campaign and category. General benchmarks:

| Category | Typical Payout Range (per 1K views) |
|---|---|
| AI Content | $2–$8 |
| Clipping / Compilation | $1.50–$5 |
| Faceless Content | $2–$6 |
| Music / Audio | $1–$4 |
| Premium Brand Campaigns | $5–$15+ |

At 3–5 videos per day across 3 channels, with each video averaging 10,000–50,000 views, a fully operational pipeline generates meaningful recurring revenue without ongoing human labor.

## Submission Best Practices

Based on the pipeline's output format and Content Rewards approval patterns:

**Video Format:** 9:16 vertical, 1080×1920, H.264, 30fps minimum. Content Rewards accepts the same export format as TikTok and YouTube Shorts.

**Caption Compliance:** All captions must match the voiceover exactly. Brands review captions for brand safety before approval.

**Hook Timing:** The brand's product or campaign theme should appear or be referenced within the first 5 seconds of content, even in faceless/clipping formats. This is a common rejection reason.

**Batch Submission:** Submit in batches of 5–10 videos per campaign rather than one at a time. Batch submissions are reviewed together and approved faster.

**Campaign Budget Monitoring:** Campaigns close when their budget is exhausted. Monitor remaining budget on active campaigns and prioritize high-budget campaigns with favorable payout rates.

## Platform Alternatives

If Content Rewards campaigns are not available in a given category, the following platforms offer similar brand-funded content programs:

| Platform | Model | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Spotter Studio** | YouTube-focused creator funding | Requires established YouTube channel |
| **Whalar** | Brand-creator matching | Requires application and approval |
| **Grin** | Influencer marketing platform | Better for accounts with existing audience |
| **YouTube BrandConnect** | YouTube-native brand deals | Requires YouTube Partner Program membership |

Content Rewards is the primary recommendation for the CorCompass pipeline because it accepts faceless and AI-generated content without requiring an existing audience.
